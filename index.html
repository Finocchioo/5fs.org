When do we meet?
We currently meet once per month.
Where do we meet?
We agree on an establishment a few weeks before the event.  That is, until we find a home location we like.  Venues typically exist somewhere between Elmhurst and Geneva.
Who is allowed to join?
We welcome all walks of life. Fortunately, we have the fortitude and wherewithal to put political differences aside and focus on those subjects of which we have direct control over. 
Are women allowed?
This is a men's group with the focus on men helping other men.  If you're a woman and have the balls to join, we're not going to say no.
What makes this men's group different?
We've noticed most men's groups are affiliated with churches. Most Online Meetup Groups are hobby focused.  We ask:  Why should we compartmentalize our interests?  Surely, a more holistic approach could be taken.  We created this group to listen to and encourage one another in the most important aspects of life.  Many support groups have a strict policy against giving out advice whereas our group encourages men to have the courage to speak up and inspire others, share stories, and stive for honor and integrity in everything we do.
What are the requirements to join?
All we ask of members is that everything we do be rooted in love, honor, and courage.  We ask that whatever entity we believe in guide us to be a blessing onto others.  We've found that those who put others first and work courageously to serve will ironically find themselves in positions of leadership.  And that is exactly the type of person we wish to forge in and help lead our men's group.
